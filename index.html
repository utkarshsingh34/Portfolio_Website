About

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Utkarsh Singh, a dedicated Product Designer and Software Engineer who loves crafting solutions that make a difference. My journey started in software engineering, where I got to grips with technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React, building scalable applications that solve real-world problems. But as I grew more into the world of tech, I discovered a passion for design—using tools like Figma and Adobe Creative Suite to create experiences that aren't just functional, but also resonate deeply with users.

I've had the chance to work on a variety of projects, from leading the design and development of AI-driven applications at Headstarter AI to working with non-profits like Hamari Silai, where I helped empower communities through technology and storytelling. These experiences have taught me the importance of blending creativity with technical skill and reinforced my belief in technology as a tool for good.

I appreciate collaboration and continuous learning, always seeking out new perspectives to deliver solutions that truly matter. Whether I'm coding a complex application or designing a seamless user interface, my goal is simple: to bridge the gap between technology and people, creating experiences that are both impactful and meaningful.

Volunteering

  • Why Volunteering Matters to Me

    Volunteering has always been more than just an activity for me—it's a way to connect with communities, learn from them, and contribute to something bigger than myself. It's about using my skills and time to make a meaningful impact on the lives of others. The sense of fulfillment that comes from knowing you've made a difference, no matter how small, is indescribable. Through volunteering, I've learned the power of empathy, collaboration, and the incredible resilience of people facing challenges. It's a reminder that we all have something to give, and together, we can create lasting change.

    Hamari Silai

    At Hamari Silai, I was moved by the resilience and determination of the women involved. Collaborating with this women's empowerment NGO allowed me to contribute to something greater than myself. Whether it was helping to introduce a new product line or documenting their inspiring stories, the impact was profound. These women, who once struggled to provide for their families, were now producing masks and other essential products during the pandemic, playing a critical role in their communities. Their stories were not just tales of survival—they were stories of empowerment, growth, and hope.

    Seva Charitable Trust

    Working with the Tribal communities in Sanjay Gandhi National Park was a transformative experience. By documenting their stories and struggles, we didn't just create a documentary; we amplified their voices. Seeing how our work brought attention and support to their cause was incredibly rewarding. It was a reminder of the power of storytelling and the impact that a dedicated team can have on raising awareness for marginalized communities.

    Yasham Foundation

    Tutoring underprivileged children at Yasham Foundation was more than just teaching; it was about building confidence and opening doors to new opportunities. Watching these kids grasp new concepts in Math and English, and seeing their faces light up with understanding, was a humbling experience. The workshops and activities we organized were not just educational—they were moments of joy and learning that helped these children believe in their potential and aspire for a brighter future.
