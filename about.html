Who am I?

The star that shines the brightest

Hi there! I'm Utkarsh Singh, a Stats and CS major at UIUC. I'm especially interested in full-stack web development, machine learning, and startups. I have a broader interest in mathematics and algorithms that underpins my technical work.

Over the last semester, I built an AI Auditor for Cloud Configuration Compliance (yes, it's a mouthful) under the guidance of Principal PM at Microsoft and developed the business platform for a UIUC based subleasing startup.

Recently, I developed the CS SAIL website. Previously, I served as Learning Assistant for University Physics Mechanics, Course Assistant for Intro to Computer Science, and as an Astronomy Tutor.

Outside of all this, I enjoy stargazing, playing chess, solving all sorts of puzzles, playing basketball, discovering new flavors of coffee, reading philosophy, eating ramen, and pushing my body to its limit on a regular basis.

P.S. - This site is an attempt to integrate myself into a digital identity. For now it serves the purpose of highlighting some of my work. In the future, I will be adding a lot more about my experiences, the thoughts that rose from them and the perspectives I gained as a result.
